## Perfect Fit Definition (My Lane)

A lead is perfect fit when:
- I can see their inquiry form / booking page and spot a gap in the follow-up
- They have visible demand (ads running, active social, reviews coming in)
- I can record a Loom in under 5 minutes pointing at something specific

## Good Fit Definition (My Lane)

A lead is good fit when:
- They look like they have leads but I can't verify the follow-up process
- They're in a high-lead industry (real estate, clinics, agencies, consultants)
- I need to ask one question before I can be specific
