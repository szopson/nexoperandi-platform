## Who Should Choose Paid Ads

**Paid ads are not the first move for most people.**

Choose paid ads when you already have:

- A locked offer
- A clear CTA (decision call or one-page offer)
- Proof that the message gets signal
- A simple funnel that works end-to-end
- The ability to respond to leads fast

**If you do not have those, paid ads will not "fix" it.**

It will just spend money faster.

**Paid ads is a multiplier. It multiplies what already works.**

---

## Why We Are Not Adding Paid Ads In Week 1

Right now, Week 1 is about locking a test you can run.

Paid ads adds too many variables too early.

So we do not add it today.

**We build the infrastructure first. Then we add ads after we have signal.**

---

## What To Do Instead (For Now)

If you want to run ads eventually, your job right now is to prepare the inputs:

1. Build the one-page offer doc
2. Install the decision call OS
3. Set up the scoreboard
4. Run outreach or content for 7 days
5. Collect signal
6. Fix one leak

**When those inputs are clean, paid ads becomes simple.**
